Caleb Adeyemi Adeleye

PhD Candidate in Applied Mathematics

University of Delaware, Newark, DE

I work on homogenization theory, multiscale analysis, PDEs, and asymptotic methods for modeling complex systems, understanding how microscopic structures drive macroscopic behavior without losing the essential physics. Looking ahead, I aim to combine classical homogenization theory with generative models and machine learning to reconstruct fine-scale structures and improve reduced-order models.
